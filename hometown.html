My Hometown

Vizag

    • Visakhapatnam, commonly known as Vizag, is a coastal city in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. The history of Visakhapatnam dates back to ancient times, and the city has played an important role in the region's political, cultural, and economic development..

    Attractions

    • Beaches: Visakhapatnam is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in India, including Ramakrishna Beach, Rushikonda Beach, and Yarada Beach. These beaches offer stunning views of the Bay of Bengal and are popular spots for swimming, sunbathing, and surfing.
    • Kailasagiri: Kailasagiri is a scenic hilltop park that offers panoramic views of the city and the sea. The park features several attractions, including a ropeway ride, a musical fountain show, and a giant statue of Lord Shiva.
    • Araku Valley: Araku Valley is a picturesque hill station located about 120 km from Visakhapatnam. The valley is known for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and coffee plantations, and is a popular destination for trekking and hiking.

    Local Cuisine

    • Seafood: As a coastal city, Visakhapatnam is known for its delicious seafood. Some of the most popular seafood dishes in the city include fish curry, prawn fry, and crab masala.
    • Pootharekulu: Pootharekulu is a sweet delicacy that is made from rice flour, sugar, and ghee. The dish is typically prepared during festivals and special occasions.
    • Ghee Roast Dosa: Ghee Roast Dosa is a popular street food in Visakhapatnam, and is made by smearing a crispy dosa with ghee and filling it with potato masala.
